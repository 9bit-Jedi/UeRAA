About Us

Developers & Designers

We are team UeRAA. We are the group of four guys who noticed that a lot of valuable of students was getting wasted in the long lines of canteens. We wondered if there is an app for everything then why can't there be one here at IIT-R which helps the students ordering their food online and then going to the canteen only when the food is prepared. So, we decided to take the initiative of developing such a software. After long discussions and hard work, the result was Canteena. We hope you guys like it.
